About The Trainer

Giles Male

Giles is a CIMA qualified management accountant and a career modeller. He is a member of FAST's Moderation Board and has helped hundreds of modellers pass FAST's Level 1 certification exam.

Over the course of his career, Giles worked on multiple bid teams in the defence and telecoms industries before going it on his own as a modelling freelancer and consultant.
