Excel Mastery Bootcamp

Sometimes you just need a quick, intense boost of training to get back up to speed

Our virtual Excel Mastery Bootcamp will leave you with the skills to build practical, professional models that follow best-practice principles

Price

$495 per person (inc VAT)

Bootcamp Structure

Blended delivery approach. Online learning, trainer-led virtual classes, practical case studies with feedback

In-house Training

This bootcamp can also be delivered face to face (UK-only). Minimum group size of 8

What You’ll Learn

The data driven approach to modelling

  • The core functions for effective modelling
  • How to build models using Excel Tables and Structured referencing
  • How to load and transform your data using PowerQuery
  • How to analyse and visualise your data using Pivot Tables and Pivot Charts
  • Modelling best practice

Excel Bootcamp

  • Trainer-led sessions (8 hours in total)
  • Exercises and case studies
  • Access to online content for 1 month
  • Supportive global community

Registration

About Trainers

Myles Arnott

Myles is a CIMA qualified accountant and has spent much of his career as a data analyst, project manager and business analyst.

He heads up the tech side of Full Stack, bringing new modelling tech innovations to the attention of the Full Stack community.

Myles also supports a number of UK businesses as a consultant Finance Director.

Giles Male

Giles is a CIMA qualified management accountant and a career modeller. He is a member of FAST's Moderation Board and has helped hundreds of modellers pass FAST's Level 1 certification exam.

Over the course of his career, Giles worked on multiple bid teams in the defence and telecoms industries before going it on his own as a modelling freelancer and consultant.
